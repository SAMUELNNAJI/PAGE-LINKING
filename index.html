Center for Media and Dialogue Initiative (CMDI)

Who We Are

Center for Media and Dialogue Initiative (CMDI) is a youth- and women-focused organization with a strong passion for promoting media, leadership development, and socioeconomic empowerment. We amplify voices and engage beneficiaries constructively through enlightenment campaigns, workshops, seminars, and conferences.
Our programs are designed to provide practical opportunities for growth, capacity building, and civic responsibility. From leadership mentorship to skills development, environmental advocacy, and community engagement, CMDIremains committed to preparing young people and women to be leaders of positive change in their communities and beyond.
